// Where It Breaks Today

Spreadsheets don't scale past one coach.

The moment a club runs multiple locations, Excel stops being a system and becomes a coordination problem.

01

No shared overview

Each coach keeps their own sheet. Club management has no single view of attendance, payments, or membership status across locations.

02

Manual everything

Attendance is logged by hand, payment status is tracked separately, and schedule changes get lost between chat groups and phone calls.

03

No structure for roles

Players, coaches, and management all need different access — but a spreadsheet can't enforce who sees or edits what.

// The Recommendation

Flutter app for the court. Web dashboard for the office.

One codebase covers iOS and Android for coaches and players in the field. A web admin dashboard gives club management the desktop-level overview they asked for — same backend, same data, always in sync.
